HOW TO PLAY:

ROLE MODEL 2048 is a musically-themed version of the classic puzzle game 2048. In this version, combine tiles featuring his albums, singles, and EPs to progress through his discography to his latest release: Kansas Anymore (The Longest Goodbye).

Just like the original 2048, combine matching tiles by pressing the arrow keys on your keyboard to slide them in that direction. When two identical tiles collide, they merge into a new one representing the next level. Keep going until you unlock all 11 covers!
